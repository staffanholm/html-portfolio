The Best Movies According to Staffan

My top 3 movies of all time

The Matrix

The ultimate story of breaking free of the system all while doing kung fu and quoting philosopers. The birth of bullet-time!

The Lord of the Rings

The journey is the goal. Just as the books set the standard for fantasy writing, this movie has been unsurpassed for the same genre in movies.

The Empire Strikes Back

The penultimate Star Wars movie. Eccentric jedi masters, exotic worlds, overcoming loss and hardship. This movie has it all.
